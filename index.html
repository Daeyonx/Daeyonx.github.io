Research

My time at Frontiers of Science institute at UNCO, where I conducted research in the field of informational technologies and computer science.

Research Paper Abstract

The Internet of Things IoT ecosystem has been expanding to 18.8 billion devices as more smart home appliances and technologies increase within homes. It's significant to understand the privacy risks and concerns that come with having IoT devices that lack data security. As a result, fuzzing was implemented as a technique that changes inputs in order to create unexpected outputs to discover possible vulnerabilities. Fuzzing through the usage of companion apps is a way to fuzz without encountering the challenges of needing an open-sourced firmware. In this survey, we address the existing companion app-based fuzzers in order to understand the effectiveness and methodology of each fuzzer, which then gives better insight to understand which fuzzers have the best functionality for certain situations
